This is the repository for the **Clinical Management System (VetService)**, a web application project focused on meeting the need for affordable, intuitive, and low-cost management tools for **small veterinary clinics and animal health businesses.**

## üéØ About the Project

Clinical management requires numerous procedures that are often neglected due to the lack of an adequate management system. Many systems available on the market are expensive, involve contractual bureaucracy, and are overly complex for small enterprises.

**VetService** was developed to overcome these barriers. By using this management tool, animal health professionals can:

* **Decrease operational costs.**
* **Increase performance and efficiency.**
* **Enhance client satisfaction** through faster and more effective service.

The result is an effective, fast, simple, and intuitive web application, making it ideal even for inexperienced users.
